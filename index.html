About Our Institute
GitaRam is a leading education group of Murshidabad district. GitaRam Institute of Management was established in 2001, affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, W.B. (Formerly known as WBUT). The Institute is located 1 km away from Berhampore town, beside NH-34 and on the bank of the river Bhagirathi. The Campus is enriched with greeneries and free from the congestion and pollution of city life.
